Coca-Cola (KO) is a Dividend King consumer staples holding with one of the deepest brand moats in global commerce. Its asset-light franchise concentrate model — serving ~225 bottling partners across 200+ countries — delivers 28-32% operating margins on ~$47B in annual revenue. The portfolio spans 200+ brands from flagship Coke CSDs to Fairlife dairy nutrition. Decentralizability scores near the floor: physical beverage production and cold-chain distribution are structurally centralized, and while open-source cola recipes (OpenCola) and home fermentation (kombucha) offer household-scale alternatives, they do not threaten commercial scale. Market cap estimated ~$260B as of early 2026.

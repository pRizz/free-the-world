Coca-Cola (KO) is a Consumer Staples Dividend King with one of the world's widest brand and distribution moats. Its asset-light concentrate model, 200+ brand portfolio, and 60+ consecutive years of dividend growth make it a benchmark defensive holding. Decentralizability is very low — beverages are physical goods anchored in cold chains and brand loyalty — though open-source recipes (OpenCola) and home fermentation (kombucha, oat milk) offer meaningful household-scale alternatives that eliminate commercial supply chain dependency. Market cap ~$260B; estimated P/E ~24x; comparable operating margins ~28-32%.

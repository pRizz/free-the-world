Costco's membership warehouse model is durable but philosophically reproducible: the core mechanism is aggregated purchasing power, which cooperatives and buying clubs have practiced for over a century. Kirkland Signature's strength derives from co-packer relationships and trusted curation rather than proprietary formulation—making it more tractable for cooperative replication than a conventional brand moat. The most viable decentralization path combines cooperative governance (NCG model), direct producer sourcing (Open Food Network), and emerging microfactory production for commodity categories. Physical logistics and SKU breadth remain genuine barriers, but the intellectual architecture of the Costco model is open-source by nature.

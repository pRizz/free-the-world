Home Depot (HD) is the world's largest home improvement retailer with ~2,300 stores, ~$160B in annual revenue, and a dominant and growing professional contractor business amplified by the 2024 ~$18B acquisition of SRS Distribution. The company scores high on moat (8/10) due to store density, supply chain scale, and deepening Pro customer relationships with high switching costs, and low on decentralizability (2/10) given structural barriers to replicating convenient physical access to bulky building materials at scale. Cooperative alternatives like Ace Hardware and community tool libraries represent partial decentralized substitutes for consumer hardware needs, while distributed energy trends create both a demand shift and a direct product opportunity within Home Depot's installation services portfolio.

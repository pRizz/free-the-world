        "Cadence is one of the core infrastructure vendors for semiconductor and electronics design, selling software and related platforms that help engineers design, verify, and analyze integrated circuits, packages, printed circuit boards, and systems. Its customer base includes chipmakers, hyperscalers, electronics manufacturers, automotive suppliers, and other organizations that would rather not discover design errors after silicon comes back from the fab.",
        "The company’s strategy is framed around “Intelligent System Design,” combining traditional EDA tools with AI-assisted workflows, verification hardware, semiconductor IP, and multiphysics system analysis. This places Cadence near the machinery room of the AI and semiconductor cycle: not usually the brand on the finished device, but often part of how the device becomes manufacturable."
